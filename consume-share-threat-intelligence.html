What consuming & sharing CTI looks like in practice

Effective cyber threat intelligence is more than subscribing to a few feeds and hoping for the best. It requires a deliberate approach to ingesting, filtering, enriching, correlating, and sharing threat data so it actually reaches the people and tools that need it.

Most organisations start with good intentions — they sign up for open-source and commercial feeds, maybe join a sharing community. But without the right platform and processes, the data piles up, analysts get overwhelmed, and intel never makes it into detection rules or incident response workflows.

Cosive helps you build a structured CTI consumption and sharing capability. We connect your feeds, enrich indicators with context, correlate across sources, and ensure actionable intel flows to your SIEM, SOAR, and security team.

The goal is intel that drives action — not just data that sits in a database.
