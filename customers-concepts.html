"Who we work with" section concepts

Four design directions for the customer industry section. Click any preview to view the full concept.

C

Horizontal Banded Rows

Each industry is presented as a full-width horizontal row with the category name and icon on the left over a photo background, and example customers flowing as text on the right. A gradient fades the photo into the content area. Compact and scannable.

D

Stacked Image Cards

Each industry is a card with a photo header (icon + category name over a darkened image) and a white body listing example customers. Cards are arranged in a 3-top, 2-bottom centred grid. Clean separation between category identity and customer detail.

Card grid layout Photo headers 3 + 2 arrangement Static / no interaction

E

Cinematic Animated Showcase

A full-bleed hero that auto-cycles through industries with crossfading backgrounds and a Ken Burns zoom effect. A top navigation strip lets users click to jump to a specific industry. Includes a progress bar showing auto-advance timing. The most visually dramatic option.

Full-bleed hero Auto-cycling animation Ken Burns effect Clickable tab nav

F

Stacked Cinematic Panels

Same visual treatment as Concept E (full-width photo panels with dark gradient overlays) but presented as a scrollable stack instead of an animated slideshow. Category name and icon on the left, example customers on the right. No JavaScript required — users simply scroll through all industries.
