"What makes us unique" card concepts

Three alternative presentations for the 5 credential items inside the frosted glass card. The surrounding design (background image, card position, glassmorphism) stays the same across all concepts.

Concept A Concept B Concept C
A

Numbered Credentials

Each item displayed with a large red number (1–5) on the left, bold keyword plus description text to the right. Subtle divider lines between items create a clean two-column feel. Communicates hierarchy and a curated, deliberate list.

Icon Tiles Grid

Each credential gets a custom icon, bold title, and one-line description, laid out in a compact 3+2 grid. No bullets — each item feels like a mini tile within the card, with thin borders and subtle background differences per tile.
